Plant Care

Care made simple

Every plant comes with our care guidance. Here are the essentials to keep your plants thriving in Kampala's climate.

☀️

Light

Most indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light — near a window but out of harsh direct sun. Kampala's bright days are excellent for most of our plants.

💧

Watering

When in doubt, water less. Most houseplants prefer to dry slightly between waterings. Overwatering is the number one cause of plant death — feel the soil first.

🌡️

Temperature

Kampala's warm, stable tropical climate is actually perfect for most houseplants. Avoid cold drafts and air conditioning vents which can stress the plants.

💨

Humidity

Many of our tropical plants love humidity. Group plants together, mist occasionally, or place on a pebble tray with water to raise ambient humidity.

🌱

Feeding

Feed with a balanced liquid fertiliser monthly during the growing season (October–April). Reduce or stop feeding during the cooler dry months.

🪴

Repotting

Most plants need repotting every 1–2 years when roots start to poke through drainage holes. Always use well-draining potting mix. We can advise on the best soil mix.
